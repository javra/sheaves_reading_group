Sheaves in Geometry and Logic

Seminar / Reading Group at Nottingham

Log of the meetings:
  • 15 Nov 2017 (0th meeting, 5 participants): We went through the table of contents, discussed the presheaf categories on page 24ff.
  • 22 Nov 2017 (1st meeting, 10 participants): Examples of topoi: presheaves, finite sets. Limits in presheaf categories.
  • 29 Nov 2017 (2nd meeting, 9 participants): Continuous \(G\)-sets, definition and first properties of subobject classifiers, subobject classifiers for \(\mathsf{Set}\), \(\mathsf{Set} \times \mathsf{Set}\) and \(G\)-sets (video).
  • 6 Dec 2017 (3rd meeting, 5 participants): Continuous actions of infinite permutation groups. Equivalence of the two definitions of subobject classifiers. Subobject classifier for \(M\)-sets (video).
  • 17 Jan 2018 (4th meeting, 9 participants): Subobject classifier for various other presheaf categories. General case of subobject classifier for presheaves (video).
  • 24 Jan 2018 (5th meeting, 7 participants): Sheaves on topological spaces, with various examples and non-examples (video).
